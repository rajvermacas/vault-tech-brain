# Memex

A hypothetical personal knowledge device proposed by [[Vannevar-Bush]] in his 1945 essay "As We May Think." Envisioned as a mechanized desk that stores books, records, and communications on microfilm, with the ability to create **associative trails** — linked paths between documents.

## Relevance to LLM Wiki

Bush's vision was closer to the [[LLM-Wiki-Pattern]] than to what the web became:
- **Private** — a personal store, not a public network.
- **Actively curated** — the user builds and maintains connections.
- **Connections as first-class** — the trails between documents are as valuable as the documents themselves.

The unsolved problem: who does the maintenance? Bush imagined the human doing it manually. The LLM Wiki pattern delegates that to the LLM.
