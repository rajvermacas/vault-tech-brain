# Vannevar Bush

American engineer and science administrator (1890-1974). Proposed the [[Memex]] in his 1945 essay "As We May Think" — a vision for a personal, mechanized knowledge store with associative trails between documents.

His ideas are a spiritual predecessor to the [[LLM-Wiki-Pattern]], which solves the maintenance problem Bush left open.
